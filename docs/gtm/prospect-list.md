## Research Notes

- **Square POS signal**: Look for Square "Order Here" links in Instagram bios, Square-branded QR codes on shop website photos, or `squareup.com` in their online ordering links.
- **Specialty drinks = customization pain**: Shops with extensive modifier menus (milk swaps, syrups, temperatures) have the highest pain around default POS ordering UX — strongest Drink-UX fit.
- **1–3 locations sweet spot**: Big enough to value consistency across locations, small enough that the owner/operator is still in the decision loop.
- **Owner research tip**: Check LinkedIn for "[Shop Name] founder/owner" or look at their Google Business listing, often lists owner name.
