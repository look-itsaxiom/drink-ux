# Drink-UX GTM — Online Communities for Outreach

This document lists online forums, groups, and communities where specialty coffee shop owners and baristas congregate. These channels are ideal for secondary outreach and market research.

---

## 1. Specialized Industry Forums

These are dedicated to coffee professionals and business owners. Ideal for finding owners looking for operational improvements.

*   **[Coffee Forums UK](https://www.coffeeforums.co.uk/):** Has a dedicated **"Coffee Industry"** section and a **Coffee Business Owner Support** sub-forum.
*   **[Barista Exchange](https://www.baristaexchange.com/):** Global community for baristas and roasters. Includes a **Sponsors Forum** for industry news and marketplace deals.
*   **[CoffeeForums.com](https://www.coffeeforums.com/):** Large community with a specific **Coffee Industry Forum** for owners and growers.

---

## 2. Reddit Communities (Subreddits)

Reddit is excellent for gathering candid feedback and identifying pain points with current POS systems.

*   **[r/barista](https://www.reddit.com/r/barista/):** Primary hub for professional baristas. Good for understanding the "barista workflow" pain points.
*   **[r/Coffee](https://www.reddit.com/r/Coffee/):** Look for the **"Inside Scoop"** threads where industry insiders discuss business operations.
*   **[r/espresso](https://www.reddit.com/r/espresso/):** Technical focus on equipment; many owners participate here.
*   **[r/roasting](https://www.reddit.com/r/roasting/):** Essential for shops that roast their own beans.

---

## 3. Real-Time Chat (Slack & Discord)

These communities allow for more casual, direct conversations with tech-forward owners.

*   **[talk.coffee](https://slofile.com/slack/talkcoffee):** A public Slack group with over 1,000 members. Channels include **#technique** and **#coffeetech**.
*   **James Hoffmann Discord:** Highly active community for technical coffee discussions (requires membership).
*   **Leaderboard Coffee Discord:** Focused on specialty coffee education and tasting.

---

## 4. Professional Networking & Social Media

*   **Facebook Groups:** 
    *   **"Coffee Shop Owners"**: Direct peer-to-peer business advice.
    *   **"Barista Guild"**: Industry-standard group for professionals.
*   **[Culinary Agents](https://culinaryagents.com/):** Professional networking for the hospitality industry.

---

## Outreach Tips for Communities

1.  **Be Transparent:** Always identify yourself as a creator of Drink-UX. Avoid "stealth" marketing.
2.  **Add Value First:** Instead of just posting a link, answer questions about Square integration, ordering UX, or mobile-first PWAs.
3.  **Ask for Feedback:** "We're building a visual drink builder for Square shops and would love to hear from owners about their current ordering pain points" is more effective than "Sign up for our app."
4.  **Respect Rules:** Always check the subreddit or forum rules regarding "Self-Promotion." Many require a specific tag or day for promotional posts.
