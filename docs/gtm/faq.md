# Drink-UX GTM — FAQ for Coffee Shop Owners

Answers to the most common questions and objections from prospects. Use these in conversations, follow-up emails, or a future sales one-pager.

---

## 1. "Do I need any technical knowledge to set this up?"

**No.** Drink-UX is designed for coffee shop owners, not developers. Setup takes less than a day — you connect your Square account through our admin portal, import your menu, and you're live. If you get stuck, we walk you through it.

---

## 2. "What does it cost?"

Pricing is based on your order volume and number of locations. We offer a **free trial period** so you can see the impact before committing. Get in touch for a custom quote — most single-location shops fall in a range that pays for itself within weeks through increased average order value.

## 3. "How does it integrate with my Square POS?"

Drink-UX connects directly to your Square account via Square's API. When a customer places an order through the Drink-UX app, it appears in your Square dashboard exactly like any other order — same workflow, same receipts, same reports. You don't need to change how you operate.

---

## 4. "We already have Square Online / a Square website. Why do we need this?"

Square Online is a generic storefront — it looks like Square, not your shop. Drink-UX is a **branded, custom ordering experience** that reflects your shop's identity and makes building a specialty drink fun and intuitive. It's the difference between a stock menu and a tailored customer experience.

---

## 5. "What if my customers aren't tech-savvy?"

The Drink-UX interface is simpler and more visual than most apps your customers already use. It's designed to be intuitive — tap your cup size, pick your base, add your modifiers, and you're done. No account required, works in any mobile browser. Customers who struggle with default POS ordering often find Drink-UX *easier*.

---

## 6. "We have Toast / Clover, not Square. Does it work?"

The initial integration focuses on Square, with Toast and Clover support on the roadmap. If you're running Toast or Clover, reach out — we'd love to understand your setup and let you know where you'd land in the rollout.

---

## 7. "How is this different from just using a QR code menu or a regular mobile app?"

A static QR code menu shows your drinks but doesn't let customers place orders. A generic mobile app builder (like GloriaFood, etc.) gives you a menu — Drink-UX gives you an **interactive drink building experience**: customers visually assemble their order, see ingredient choices render in real time, and submit directly to your POS. It's engagement, not just information.

---

## 8. "What happens if the app goes down or has a problem?"

Your Square POS continues to work normally — Drink-UX is an *additional* ordering channel, not a replacement. If our service has an issue, your staff can still take orders through Square as usual. We maintain 99.9% uptime and provide support channels for any issues.

---

## 9. "My menu changes seasonally. Is that a pain to manage?"

Menu updates are handled through the Drink-UX admin portal — same concept as managing a website. Add a new seasonal drink, toggle items on/off, update prices. Changes go live immediately. You don't need to contact us or wait for a developer.

---

## 10. "Can I see what it looks like with our actual menu before deciding?"

Yes. We offer a **personalized demo** where we mock up Drink-UX using your actual menu and branding. You get to see exactly what your customers would experience before you sign anything. Just reach out and we'll set it up.
