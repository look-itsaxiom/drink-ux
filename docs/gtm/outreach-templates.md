## Notes on Template Use

- **Email (Template A)**: Best for shops where you've found the owner's direct email. Reference something specific about their shop in the first line.
- **Instagram DM (Template B)**: Best for shops with active IG presence and DMs open. Keep it short — long DMs get skipped. The demo link does the selling.
- **LinkedIn (Template C)**: Best for shops where the owner/manager has a LinkedIn profile and appears active. Slightly more formal tone.
- **Follow-up**: Send once, 7 days after initial. Don't send a third message — mark as cold and move on.
