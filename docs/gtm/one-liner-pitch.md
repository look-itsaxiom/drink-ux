# Drink-UX GTM — One-Liner Pitch

## Primary (recommended)

> **"Drink-UX gives your coffee shop a custom ordering app that makes picking a drink as fun as ordering at a game — and it plugs right into your Square POS."**

---

## Variants (context-specific)

**At a coffee industry event / trade show:**
> "We built a mobile drink builder for specialty shops — customers customize visually instead of clicking through a generic Square grid, and orders go straight to your POS."

**Casual in-person (at the shop itself):**
> "My company makes a custom ordering app for coffee shops — it replaces the default Square menu with something way more visual and interactive. Your customers actually enjoy placing the order."

**When talking to a non-technical owner:**
> "Think of it as swapping out your default Square menu for a beautiful branded ordering experience — we handle all the setup."

**When asked "how is it different from Square Online?":**
> "Square Online is a generic storefront. Drink-UX is a custom-branded drink builder — your customers build their order visually, ingredient by ingredient, on their phone. It's a completely different experience."

---

## Key Value Props to Weave In (choose 1–2 per conversation)

1. **Custom brand experience** — looks like *your* shop, not Square's
2. **Works with existing Square setup** — no rip-and-replace, integrates in a day
3. **Specialty drink complexity made easy** — visual modifiers mean fewer order errors
4. **Increases engagement / AOV** — interactive builders nudge customers to explore more options
5. **No technical knowledge required** — owner manages it through an admin dashboard
