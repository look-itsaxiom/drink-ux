## Sequence Structure

Each segment gets a 3-step sequence:
- **Step 1:** Cold intro (demo invite, no hard pitch)
- **Step 2:** Follow-up with live demo link (once SKI-17 is live)
- **Step 3:** Post-demo follow-up with trial offer

Sequences are tuned per segment based on owner persona and channel.
